This is the subversive discourse in the post-modern universe.
In this discourse, Lacan's original reversal of the subject ($) and the master (S1) results in a form of "revolutionary university", compared to the Discourse of the University in the master's universe: instead of S1 setting the agenda for the search of new knowledge (addressing a – here the unsymbolised/Real), the agenda is set by the subject itself. The result is a new master.
If the master in the post-modern universe is jouissance, this discourse is questioning not only jouissance as meaning, but this level of meaning itself – "we must strive for higher goals" – reviving ideology, grand narratives, etc.
